Python 3.4.1 was released on May 18th, 2014, with over three hundred bugfixes and other improvements over 3.4.0. One notable change: the version of OpenSSL bundled with the Windows installer no longer has the HeartBleed vulnerability. More details are available in [the change log](https://docs.python.org/3.4/whatsnew/changelog.html#python-3-4-1).
